Children’s Picture Book

Emery's Light: What’s That Feeling, Josie?

Emery’s Light is a gentle watercolor picture book series created to help young children understand emotions through simple language, warm storytelling, and sensory awareness. In Book One, “What’s That Feeling, Josie?”, a curious toddler begins noticing big feelings rising inside her. Emery appears as a supportive older sister who helps her identify what she feels, breathe through overwhelming moments, and return to connection.

The story blends emotional literacy, sibling bonding, and early self-regulation in a developmentally appropriate way. It uses soft illustrations, grounding language, and a steady rhythm to bring calm to common toddler experiences.

Series Vision

Emery’s Light is designed as a multi book series, each centered on a different emotional experience that toddlers commonly encounter.

Future books explore themes such as frustration, boundaries, separation anxiety, sibling connection, and sensory overwhelm. Each installment follows the same heart centered structure: Emery models emotional awareness, gentle language, and grounded support that children can easily imitate.

Book One serves as the primary example for this portfolio, showcasing your approach to early emotional education through storytelling and illustration.
