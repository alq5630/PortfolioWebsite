My design philosophy

I design for the nervous system first and the gradebook second. My goal is to create learning experiences that are rigorous without being overwhelming and structured without feeling rigid. I rely on clear objectives, chunked content, varied practice, and reflective questions that help learners integrate what they are studying into their actual lives.

Anchors I return to

  • Psychological safety and trauma aware framing
  • Evidence informed content and transparent sources
  • Multiple ways to engage and demonstrate understanding
  • Accessible language without losing complexity
  • Feedback loops that invite curiosity rather than shame
