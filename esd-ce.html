Continuing Education Course

ESD Method 12 Hour CE Training

This twelve hour continuing education course introduces massage therapists and bodyworkers to the Empathy Spectrum Disorder (ESD) framework and the ESD Method. It is designed to help practitioners understand empathy subtypes, nervous system patterns, and ethical boundaries while staying grounded in practical bodywork applications.

The course combines lecture, guided reflection, case study discussion, and simple practice tools that therapists can bring directly into their sessions. It honors the emotional weight that comes with caring for highly sensitive clients and offers structure that keeps both client and practitioner safer.

Course Snapshot

Who This Course Serves

  • Licensed massage therapists and bodyworkers who work with emotionally complex clients.
  • Practitioners who notice their own empathic flooding or exhaustion after sessions.
  • Instructors and clinic supervisors who want a framework for teaching emotional hygiene and boundaries.

Structure and Format

  • Total of twelve contact hours.
  • Can run as a two day intensive, three half day modules, or blended format.
  • Mix of lecture, reflection, pair work, and applied case discussion.
  • Includes printable tools for both students and instructors.
