Curriculum Design

Massage Therapy Curriculum

This project is a full course build for a college level massage therapy program. I designed the structure, learning objectives, routines, assessments, lecture supports, guided meditations, and digital materials used throughout the term.

The goal of the curriculum is to help students move from basic anatomy knowledge into confident, safe, and emotionally aware hands on practice. Each component is designed to support different learning styles while keeping the classroom grounded, clear, and regulated.

My Role

Responsibilities

  • Designed the course structure and weekly outline for the term.
  • Created written routines for full body and prenatal massage sequences.
  • Developed lecture note guides, study packets, and exam supports.
  • Built a routine workbook to help students break down and practice each sequence.
  • Recorded and scripted guided muscle meditations to connect anatomy with felt movement.
  • Designed slide decks for topics such as lymphatic drainage.
  • Recorded short demonstration videos for key routines.

Focus Areas

  • Clear structure so students always knew what to practice and why.
  • Emotional safety and nervous system regulation within the classroom.
  • Bridging cognitive understanding with hands on skill and body awareness.
  • Supports for test anxiety and information overload.
  • Printable, reusable materials that students could study from at home.
