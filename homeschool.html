Long Range Curriculum Design

Homeschool Curriculum

This homeschool project is a twelve year curriculum arc that centers emotional intelligence, sound and silence exploration, creative arts, and practical life skills.

The work includes a long range roadmap, a detailed first year plan, activity guides, emotional intelligence scaffolding, and practical arts outlines. It is built to feel gentle, rhythmic, and realistic for a real family while still giving a clear sense of direction and developmental flow.

My Role

Responsibilities

  • Outlined a twelve year homeschool journey that moves from sensory exploration toward more abstract thinking and responsibility.
  • Designed Year 1 around a Sound and Silence theme that anchors weekly activities and gentle exposure to learning routines.
  • Created an emotional intelligence framework that weaves regulation, naming feelings, and healthy boundaries into daily life.
  • Defined life skills and practical arts areas such as cooking, cleaning, gardening, and basic repair tasks.
  • Developed activity guides, checklists, and templates that turn big ideas into simple, doable steps for each day.
  • Referenced state requirements and translated them into flexible, family friendly plans.

Focus Areas

  • Emotional safety and nervous system awareness as the foundation for all learning.
  • Simple, sensory based experiences that help a young child tune into sound, stillness, and their own body.
  • Natural opportunities to practice independence, contribution, and care for the home.
  • Documentation that is clear enough for an evaluator but soft enough not to overwhelm a parent.
